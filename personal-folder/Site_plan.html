Cybersecurity Basics

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks, unauthorized access, and damage. In today’s increasingly digital world, understanding cybersecurity basics is crucial to protecting personal information, securing data, and maintaining privacy.

  • Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability (CIA Triad): Ensures sensitive data access, data consistency, and system availability.
  • Authentication and Authorization: Verifies user identity and grants resource access based on permissions.
  • Encryption: Transforms data to coded formats to prevent unauthorized access.
